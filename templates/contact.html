Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about TechEdu Hub

How quickly do you respond to support requests?
We aim to respond to all support requests within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent technical issues, our AI chatbot provides immediate assistance 24/7.
Is TechEdu Hub free for students?
Yes! We offer a comprehensive free tier that includes basic chatbot support, access to learning resources, and community features. Premium plans are available for enhanced features.
Can institutions integrate TechEdu Hub?
Absolutely! We offer institutional plans with custom integrations, branding options, and dedicated support for educational organizations.
What types of technical issues can you help with?
We assist with hardware troubleshooting, software installation, network connectivity, programming questions, database queries, and general IT support for students.
Do you offer phone support?
Phone support is available for premium subscribers and institutional clients. Free users can access our comprehensive chat support and email assistance.
How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?
You can report bugs or suggest features through our contact form, email, or directly through the feedback option in your account dashboard.
